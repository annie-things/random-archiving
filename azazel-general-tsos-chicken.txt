The best General Tso's recipe ever. Enough for 4 people easily.

1100 Grams Chicken

BATTER
6 eggs
1 cup Corn Flour/Starch

SAUCE
15 tbls Soy Sauce
15 tbls Sugar
8.5 tbls White Wine(not too sweet)
7.5 tbls Seasoned Rice vinegar
10 teas Corn Flour/Starch


Mix all ingrediants for sauce mix until the sugar and corn flour is dissolved.

Beat the eggs in a large bowl while adding corn flour until it makes a nice lovely yellowish batter.

Cut chicken into cubes(size is up to you) and add to the batter.

Heat a larg amount of oil in a wok or deep fat friar and drop chicken in small bathes and remove any excess from the pan until the chicken is cooked completely.

Once all chicken is cook add to the wok(without the oil) and add the sauce on a low heat and cook for 10+ minutes until all or the majority of the sauce is carmalized.

Serve on a bed of rice with broccoli.
